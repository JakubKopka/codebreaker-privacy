Privacy Policy

Codebreaker

Effective date: July 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. The game Codebreaker does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

📌 What data is stored?

Codebreaker stores only basic game progress locally on your device:

This data is not transmitted to any server and is not shared with third parties.
